Legible Sydney
City of Sydney Wayfinding Strategy

Working with Urban & Public a part of ASPECT Studios in collaboration with Studion Binocular we created a wayfinding strategy 'Legible Sydney' for the City of Sydney. We developed the messaging and mapping and refined the designs of the initial pilot project. Also provided was comprehensive documentation, site auditing, sign removal audits, detailed mapping, illustration, typography and finished artwork for more than 700 individual sites.
